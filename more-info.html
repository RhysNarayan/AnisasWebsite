    Our Special Spots

    The place we walked to on our first date, where time stood still as we shared our first kiss. It was there that I knew, with all my heart, you were the one meant for me.

    Our special spot—the place where we shared our first tray of donuts. Though it felt crowded at first, the moment we sat down, it was just us, gazing at the ocean, as if the world disappeared and it was you and me against everything.

    Our lazy spot—the place we escape to when we’re too tired to walk, where we sit in the car, listen to music, and share stories. It’s where we laugh and enjoy the comfort of each other’s company as we unwind and relax.

    Our Go To Spots For Food

    McDonald's—our go-to spot when we’re hungry and need a quick meal. You’re always indecisive, but that’s what I love about you. Even after wandering for hours, feet aching, you’ll still stand there, debating, only to settle on our usual.

    La Parada—our favorite spot when we’re over McDonald’s. It’s our go to for sushi but better known as the place you always paying for me. The place where we eat, laugh, and snap a million Instagram photos.

    Doxa Donuts—our perfect substitute for Dinky Donuts, just as good. The treat we take on our little beach dates, indulging as we sit on our benches, gazing out at the ocean.
